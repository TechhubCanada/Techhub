## About Agency by Naman Kataria

Agency by Naman Kataria is a Toronto-based web development and design agency founded by Naman Kataria. The agency focuses on modern digital experiences, including custom websites, ecommerce platforms, UI/UX design, React and Next.js development, SEO-friendly builds, API integrations, and website redesigns.

For this project, the agency is responsible for redesigning the Tech Hub Canada ecommerce experience with a practical, modern storefront that supports product discovery, online shopping, service visibility, and long-term digital growth.
